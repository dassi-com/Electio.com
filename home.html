A Remote Election Management & Voting System

Are you looking for an Election Solution?
Ma-Poule App innovative solution managementfor remote election and voting addresses the issues of transparency, efficiency, and accessibility within the electoral process, by providing a secure and reliable platform for remote voting, election management & tallying. A solution that is not just a Voting Website, get an Election Management Portal, Voters Portal (Web-vote), USSD-Voting and Realtime Election results Webpage. Ma-Poule Voting software is also integrated with SMS & EMAIL for communication to voters to aid in increasing their participation, Blockchain-based VVPAT, and addition of a Soft-ballot printing system to ensure a verifiable election
